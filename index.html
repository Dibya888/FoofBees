WHAT WE DO?

1. We are dedicated to help unpriviledged people
2. We collect leftover foods and other donations
3. Our volunteers distribute those items to poor people

ITEMS COLLECTION

We collect leftover foods and other items
from those who want to donate by contacting
us.

PROCESSING

After collecting items from donors, our volunteers then processes those items to become reusable or edible to eat.

DISTRIBUTION

Within a short period of time, our volunteers redistribute those among unpriviledged people in different areas.

Features

  • PEOPLE SERVED

    We have served around 250 unpreviledged people around Dhaka with foods and cloths.

  • DONORS REGISTERED

    We have recieved donation from 50 registered donors including restaurent and event owners

  • VOLUNTEERS

    Currently there are 20 volunteers in our team to collect and distribute items.
